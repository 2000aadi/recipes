Mexican Veg Lasagne

Description

A vegetarian Tex-Mex lasagna made with corn tortillas and packed with cheese and veggies. Garnish with cilantro, sour cream, and other favorite toppings.

Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced sweet onion
  • 1 jalapeno peppers, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded pepper Jack cheese, divided
  • 1 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 9 corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup enchilada sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 deg C. Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, jalapenos, bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
    Reduce heat to medium-low and add salsa, black beans, corn, chilli powder, and cumin. Bring to a simmer; let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine 2 cups of Jack cheese, ricotta cheese, spinach, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Place 6 corn tortillas in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Add 1/2 of the vegetable mixture using a slotted spoon, followed by 1/2 of cheese and spinach mixture.
    Add a layer of 6 tortillas and follow with remaining vegetables and remaining cheese mixture. Top with remaining tortillas. Spread enchilada sauce across the top and remaining pepper Jack cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
